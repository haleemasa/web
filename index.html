I completed my PhD in Education with focus on "Female Pakistani students' identities in transition to UK higher education" from University of Manchester, UK in 2020. My PhD is a longitudinal study, which is based on a socio-cultural theoretical framework using photo elicitation, timeline, concept mapping and narrative methodology.

I developed my research and analytical skills in the six MSc Education modules. I secured more than 60 % marks in quantitative data generation, qualitative data generation, critical reading and writing, planning of research, qualitative data analysis and quantitative data analysis.

I have more than 20 years of professional experience in teaching study skills, educational research methods, theories of teaching and learning, project management and in academic supervision of undergraduate and postgraduate students at various academic institutions of the UK and abroad.
